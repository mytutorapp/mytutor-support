Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

My Tutor respects your privacy. This app does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information for tracking or advertising purposes.

Information Collection

My Tutor does not collect, store, or share personal data such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

User-generated questions and responses are processed solely to provide educational answers within the app. This data is not linked to a personal identity.

Subscriptions

Payments are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. The developer does not receive or store payment details.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple-provided frameworks and secure API services to generate responses. No personal user profiles are created.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect data and ensure secure communication.

Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for general educational use and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
