Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

My Tutor is designed to support learning while respecting user privacy.

Information We Do Not Collect

My Tutor does not require account creation and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, or advertising identifiers.

The app does not track users across apps or websites and does not sell personal data.

Information Sent to Third-Party AI Service

When a user submits a question in the app, the following information is transmitted securely to a third-party AI provider (OpenAI) for the sole purpose of generating an educational response:

  • The text of the user’s question
  • The selected teaching style or tutor mode
  • Recent conversation messages (if part of the active chat session)

This information is sent only when the user taps the send button. No background transmission occurs.

No account identifiers, contact details, or device identifiers are attached to these requests.

Purpose of Data Processing

The transmitted information is used exclusively to generate tutoring responses within the app. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

Data Storage

Chat messages are stored locally on the user’s device during use of the app. The developer does not maintain a separate user database of personal identities.

Payments

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. The developer does not receive or store payment card details.

Children’s Privacy

My Tutor is designed for general educational use. Users are advised not to submit sensitive personal information in chat messages.

Security

All communication with third-party services uses encrypted connections (HTTPS).
